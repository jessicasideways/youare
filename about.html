About the You Are Theme

The You Are Theme is a WordPress theme framework in development and is consistently evolving to meet the needs of people interested in a personal website.

You Are vs. YouAre

The You Are Theme is a continuation from the YouAre Theme. The YouAre Theme was a very simple WordPress theme that was good at what it was billed for: minimalism. The You Are Theme takes that idea and puts what is important to the site owner first. If you want more information on the classic YouAre theme, it is available but we are treating it as a legacy product more than anything else.
